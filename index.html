Game instructions

The game consists of writing words starting with the selected letter for each category.

At the top is the "get started" button that starts the game. At first you will need to enter your username.

You will have choice between 2 category options:

  • standard 5 categories
  • own categories choosen from 5 standard ones
and 3 dificult levels:
  • easy
  • medium
  • hard

Difficult level can be changed after each round. Changing category will reset your game.

You will play against the computer. You'll choose the letter alternately. Once used, the letter cannot be used again.

At the end of every round you will see results and points in the table card game.

At the end of the game you will see the total points and overview.

When you get started the instruction will be hidden, but don't worry you will found it in donw of the site.

Enjoy and ... a lot of words.
